### For Homeowners

**Q: How do I post a job?**
A: From the home screen, tap "Post a Job" and choose your method:
1. **Video:** Record a 60-second video of the project area
2. **Photo:** Upload photos of what needs work
3. **Text:** Describe your project in detail

Our AI will analyze your submission and generate an instant quote.

**Q: How accurate are the AI quotes?**
A: Our AI quotes are approximately 97% accurate, based on analysis of millions of completed projects. The system learns from every job, continuously improving its predictions.

**Q: How do I find a contractor?**
A: After posting a job, you'll see a list of matched contractors in your area. Each profile shows their:
- Overall rating and reviews
- Verification status
- Years of experience
- Response time
- Match score for your specific job

**Q: How are payments protected?**
A: All payments are held in secure escrow until the job is completed to your satisfaction. You only release payment when you're happy with the work. We use Stripe for payment processing, which is PCI DSS compliant.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the work?**
A: Contact our support team immediately. We'll work with you and the contractor to resolve any issues. Funds remain in escrow until disputes are resolved.

### For Contractors

**Q: How do I sign up as a contractor?**
A: Download the app and select "I'm a Contractor" during signup. You'll need to provide:

- Background check consent
- Bank account for payouts

**Q: How much does it cost to be on the platform?**

**Q: How do I get more jobs?**
A: To improve your job matching:

- Upload portfolio photos
- Respond to inquiries quickly
- Maintain high ratings
- Keep your schedule updated

**Q: How do payouts work?**
A: Once a homeowner releases payment, funds are transferred to your connected bank account within 2-3 business days. You can track all earnings in the app.

**Q: Can I set my own rates?**
A: Yes! While our AI provides quote suggestions, you can adjust your rates when bidding on jobs. Your pricing autonomy is part of our Fair Trade model.

### Account & Settings

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.

**Q: How do I update my profile?**
A: Go to Profile > Edit Profile. You can update your photo, contact information, bio, and service areas.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Any pending transactions must be completed first.

**Q: How do I change notification settings?**
A: Go to Settings > Notifications. You can customize which alerts you receive via push notification, email, and SMS.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app won't load. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Force close and restart the app
3. Clear app cache (Settings > Clear Cache)
4. Update to the latest version
5. Reinstall the app

If issues persist, contact support with your device info and error messages.

**Q: Why isn't my location showing correctly?**
A: Ensure location services are enabled for FairTradeWorker (Settings > Privacy > Location Services). If still inaccurate, try restarting your device.

**Q: Photos/videos aren't uploading. Help!**
A: Check these common issues:
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Reduce file size if over 50MB
- Grant photo library permissions
- Try switching between WiFi and cellular

**Q: I'm getting error messages. What do they mean?**
A: Common error codes:
- **ERR_AUTH_001:** Session expired. Please log in again.
- **ERR_PAY_002:** Payment failed. Check card details.
- **ERR_JOB_003:** Job submission failed. Try again.
- **ERR_NET_004:** Network error. Check connection.

For unresolved errors, screenshot the message and send to support.
