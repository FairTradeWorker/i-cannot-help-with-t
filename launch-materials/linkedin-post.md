### Post 2: The Economics

I've been asked: "How can you give contractors zero fees?"

Let me break down the math.

**Traditional Platform Model:**
- Homeowner pays $10,000 for roof
- Platform takes 20% ($2,000)

- Platform needs high volume to survive

**FairTradeWorker Model:**
- Homeowner pays $10,000 for roof

The difference: $1,200 MORE per job for the contractor.
