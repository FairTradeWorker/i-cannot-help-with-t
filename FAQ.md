## General Questions

### What is FairTradeWorker?

FairTradeWorker is a revolutionary platform that connects homeowners directly with trusted contractors through AI-powered job matching. We eliminate the middleman, creating fair opportunities for skilled tradespeople while providing homeowners with transparent pricing and quality service.

**Our mission**: *Death of the Middleman. Birth of the Trade Infrastructure.*

### Who is FairTradeWorker for?

- **Homeowners** looking to hire contractors for home improvement projects
- **Contractors** seeking fair-paying jobs without middleman fees
- **Territory Operators** wanting to earn passive income by managing local markets
- **Businesses** needing access to trade industry intelligence and APIs

### When did FairTradeWorker launch?

FairTradeWorker launched on **November 27, 2025**.

### Is FairTradeWorker available in my area?

We're currently expanding across the United States through our Territory system. Check our [Territory Map](https://fairtradeworker.com/territories) to see if your ZIP code is covered. If not, consider becoming a Territory Operator!

### How is FairTradeWorker different from HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack, etc.?

| Feature | FairTradeWorker | Traditional Platforms |
|---------|----------------|----------------------|
| Contractor fees | Minimal platform fee | High lead fees ($15-100+) |
| Pricing transparency | Full visibility | Hidden markups |
| AI matching | Advanced algorithms | Basic matching |
| Territory ownership | Yes | No |
| Video job posting | Yes | Limited |
| Real-time bidding | Yes | No |

### Is there a mobile app?

Yes! FairTradeWorker is available on:

The mobile app has full feature parity with the web application.

## For Homeowners

### How do I post a job?

1. **Sign up** for a free homeowner account
2. **Create a job** using text, photo, or video description
3. **Receive bids** from verified contractors
4. **Compare** estimates and contractor profiles
5. **Accept** the best bid and get work done

### Is it free to post jobs?

Yes! Posting jobs is completely free for homeowners. You only pay for the work once you accept a bid and the job is completed.

### How do I pay for work?

FairTradeWorker uses secure escrow payments through Stripe:

1. Funds are held securely when you accept a bid
2. Contractor completes the work
3. You approve the work completion
4. Funds are released to contractor

This protects both homeowners and contractors.

### What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

We have a comprehensive **Dispute Resolution** system:

1. First, communicate directly with your contractor
2. If unresolved, open a dispute through the platform
3. Our team reviews evidence from both parties
4. Fair resolution is determined

Most issues are resolved within 5-7 business days.

### Can I choose my contractor?

Absolutely! You have full control:
- Browse contractor profiles
- View ratings and reviews
- Check verification status
- Compare multiple bids
- Choose based on your preferences

### What types of jobs can I post?

Any home improvement or trade work, including:
- Plumbing
- Electrical
- HVAC
- Roofing
- Painting
- Carpentry
- Landscaping
- General repairs
- Renovations
- And more!

## For Contractors

### How do I become a contractor on FairTradeWorker?

1. **Sign up** for a contractor account
2. **Complete verification** (license, insurance, background check)
3. **Set up your profile** with specialties and service area
4. **Start bidding** on available jobs

### What are the fees for contractors?

Our fee structure is transparent and fair:
- **No lead fees** - Unlike competitors, we don't charge per lead
- **Small platform fee** - Only charged on completed jobs
- **No subscription required** - Pay only when you get paid

This results in significantly more earnings compared to traditional platforms.

### How do I get paid?

Payments are processed through Stripe:

1. Job is completed and approved by homeowner
2. Funds are released from escrow
3. Direct deposit to your bank account
4. Typically arrives within 2-3 business days

### How does the bidding system work?

1. Browse available jobs in your area
2. View job details and requirements
3. Submit your bid with price and message
4. Homeowner reviews and may accept
5. You're notified when your bid is accepted

### Can I see job videos before bidding?

Yes! Homeowners can post 60-second videos describing their jobs. This helps you:
- Accurately assess the work
- Provide better estimates
- Save time on site visits

### What is Route Optimization?

Our AI-powered Route Optimization helps you:
- Plan efficient job schedules
- Minimize travel time between jobs
- Maximize daily earnings
- Reduce fuel costs

### Do I need to claim a territory?

No, territories are optional. You can bid on any job in your service area. However, Territory Operators get additional benefits and priority placement.

## For Territory Operators

### What is a Territory Operator?

Territory Operators are exclusive local market managers who:
- Claim specific ZIP code territories
- Earn passive income from territory activity
- Get priority in contractor matching
- Access advanced analytics and insights

### What is the First 300 Program?

The First 300 Program offers exclusive benefits to our earliest Territory Operators:
- **Lower pricing** - Locked-in early adopter rates
- **Larger territories** - More ZIP codes per claim
- **Lifetime benefits** - Special recognition and perks
- **Priority support** - Direct access to our team

Only 300 spots available!

### How do I claim a territory?

1. **Check availability** on our Territory Map
2. **Choose your ZIP codes** (based on subscription tier)
3. **Complete payment** through Stripe
4. **Start managing** your territory

### How much does it cost?

Territory pricing varies by location and tier:

| Tier | ZIP Codes | Monthly Cost |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| Starter | 1-5 | Starting at $49/mo |
| Professional | 5-15 | Starting at $149/mo |
| Enterprise | 15+ | Custom pricing |

First 300 operators get significant discounts!

### How do Territory Operators earn money?

- **Activity fees** - Small percentage of jobs in your territory
- **Advertising** - Local business promotions
- **Analytics** - Sell market insights (Enterprise tier)
- **Referrals** - Bonus for bringing new contractors

## Technical Questions

### What technology does FairTradeWorker use?

**Frontend**:
- React 19 with TypeScript
- Vite build system
- Tailwind CSS styling
- shadcn/ui components

**Mobile**:
- React Native with Expo
- NativeWind (Tailwind)
- React Navigation

**Backend**:
- Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
- Stripe (payments)
- Upstash Redis (caching)
- Vercel (serverless functions)

## Security & Privacy

### How is my data protected?

We take security seriously:
- **Encryption** - All data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- **Authentication** - JWT-based secure authentication
- **Rate limiting** - Protection against abuse
- **Regular audits** - Security reviews and updates

### What personal information do you collect?

We collect only what's necessary:
- Contact information (name, email, phone)
- Location data (for job matching)
- Payment information (handled by Stripe)
- Usage data (to improve the platform)

See our full [Privacy Policy](https://fairtradeworker.com/privacy) for details.

### Do you sell my data?

**Never.** We don't sell personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide and improve FairTradeWorker services.

### How are contractors verified?

Our verification process includes:
- **Identity verification** - Government ID check
- **License verification** - Trade license confirmation
- **Insurance verification** - Liability insurance check
- **Background check** - Criminal history review (where permitted)

### What if I suspect fraud?

Report immediately:
1. Use the "Report" button on the platform
2. Email: security@fairtradeworker.com
3. Call: (888) FAIR-TRADE

We investigate all reports promptly.

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We use **Stripe** for payment processing:
- PCI-DSS Level 1 certified
- No card data stored on our servers
- 3D Secure for additional protection
- Fraud detection algorithms
